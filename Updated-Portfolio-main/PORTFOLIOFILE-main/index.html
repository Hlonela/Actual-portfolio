About Me

Who Am I?

I am a 23 year old black female living in Cape Town. I enjoy doing research work, meaning, collecting data, analysing it and drawing findings from it. I'm particularly interested in studying societal issues and coming up with solutions to them. This interest led me to completing a Social Work degree through the University of Cape Town (UCT).

Why Coding?

The world is becoming more digital. Real world changers ought to digitally enclined in their attempt to come up with solutions. The fourth industrial revolution (4IR) is becoming an essential role-player in the creation of effecient solutions. Consequently, I aim to be part of the fore front leaders in bringing the necessary solutions.

MY GOAL

In my journey of becoming a web developer, my keen interest to Python has been keepin me glued to my screen. I enjoy building a solution to a problem from scratch and seeing the code running, gives me great fulfilment. I am working towards strengthening my back-end skills in coding, to be able to create effecient applications that will assist, especially, the underprivileged communities.
